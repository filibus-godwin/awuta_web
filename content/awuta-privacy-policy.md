## 1. INTRODUCTION

Awuta (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is a platform designed to help small businesses gain visibility by creating public vendor profiles linked to WhatsApp and social media platforms, integrating location features to recommend nearby vendors.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information when you use the Awuta website or mobile application.

---

## 2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### a. Information You Provide

When you create a vendor profile or use Awuta, we may collect:

- Business name  
- Profile photo or logo  
- Business description  
- WhatsApp phone number  
- Social media links (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, etc.)  
- Email address (if provided)

### b. Automatically Collected Information

We may collect certain technical information, including:

- Device type and operating system  
- App usage and interaction data  
- Log files and timestamps  
- Analytics data (e.g., via Supabase)

---

## 3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and display vendor profiles  
- Allow customers to discover and contact vendors  
- Improve app functionality and user experience  
- Monitor usage, performance, and trends  
- Display advertisements within the app  
- Communicate important updates or service notices  

We do **not** sell users’ personal information.

---

## 4. AI-ASSISTED FEATURES AND THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Awuta uses artificial intelligence (AI) tools provided by third-party service providers to assist vendors in generating descriptions for items posted for sale.

- These AI tools may process information submitted by vendors, including item titles, images, and descriptions, solely for the purpose of generating suggested content.  
- Awuta does not use personal data submitted for AI-assisted features to train its own AI models.  
- Where third-party AI services are used, data is processed in accordance with the provider’s privacy and security obligations.  
- AI-generated content is intended as a suggestion only. Vendors remain responsible for reviewing, editing, and approving all content before publishing.  
- Awuta does not sell personal data used in AI-assisted features.  
- Awuta does not control, audit, or independently verify the internal operation of third-party AI systems.
